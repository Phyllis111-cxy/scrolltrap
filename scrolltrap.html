Phase 3 of 3 · Reflection

Now step
outside the feed.

The feed is designed to keep you inside it. These three questions ask you to look at it from the outside — as a critical observer of your own experience.

Question 01

When you felt the urge to like a video, what did that moment feel like? Were you aware it might be an ad?

Think about the feeling before the decision — not what you decided.

Question 02

Looking at the reveal, which persuasion tactic surprised you most — and why did it work (or not) on you?

Reference the specific technique by name if you can.

Question 03

Name one specific signal you will watch for the next time you scroll your real For You page.

Be concrete — a word, a pattern, a visual cue.
